Event Overview

The DXC-GDN-SAP Nexus Hub Townhall is a global gathering of employees, both in person and virtually,
to discuss the vision and growth strategy for FY25.
It’s an opportunity for interacting with DXC SAP Leadership team
and get a direction for FY25!

Key Themes

FY25 Vision

The townhall will focus on the company’s vision
for the upcoming fiscal year. Leaders will share insights into strategic goals, market trends, and how the organization plans to evolve

Growth Strategy

Participants will learn about growth initiatives, expansion plans, and key performance indicators.
The goal is to understand growth strategy and align ourselves to
contribute actively to the company’s growth journey.

Collaboration

Emphasize the importance of cross-functional collaboration.
Teams working together can achieve more significant results than individual efforts.
You may look forward to connecting, sharing ideas, and exploring synergies.
